The Medium Man

“It’s only when you look at an ant through a magnifying glass on a sunny day that you realize how often they burst into flames.” – Harry Hill

The style for a lonesome night wandering the streets. Tough enough to not be mugged, yet soft enough to not appear homeless. It's rugged, but drinkable, like a slightly aged glass of wine - elegant, but unrefined.

 
 

The Blank Slate

“Cleaning up with children around is like shoveling during a blizzard.” – Margaret Culkin Banning

A go to cut to get into the Japanese bathhouse of your dreams. Suave & smooth, like a silk wrapped baby piglet. For those who wish to recover from a streak of bad luck, rise up from a sunken pit of despair, or travel back in time to age 11, the hairs hold the struggles of humanity - take them off and remember what sunshine really feels like.

 
 

The Middle Man

“I’m too drunk to taste this chicken.” – Colonel Sanders

The style that melts in your mouth, not in your hands. It often doesn't stay long, because it's embarrassing - but it is sure to leave a lasting impression in pictures.

 
 

Aged to Perfection

“You know you’re getting old when you stoop to tie your shoelaces and wonder what else you could do while you’re down there.” – George Burns

Classy without context, white hair is the new rage everyone is talking about and people all over the world are racing to one day achieve. It doesn't matter who you are or what color your skin, white facial hair instantly demands respect and can even score you some store discounts.

 
 

The Bush Boss

“To those of you who received honours, awards and distinctions, I say well done. And to the C students, I say you, too, can be president of the United States.” – George W. Bush

Calling all savages, the facial hair style you've been seeking is here. With daft and punk and sugary sweet density, you will have a bro comment or two in no time, though you may be dismissed early at family gatherings and formal events. Fear not, for secret powers of seduction and financial success lie deep, deep, deeep within this beard, deep within...
